Eight dead in wall collapse at Senegal football stadium
Eight people have died and at least 49 are injured after a wall collapsed at a football stadium in Senegal.The wall collapsed as people prepared to leave after the game.Fighting started between rival fans and police responded by using tear gas, which led to panic, a stampede, and the wall collapse.Sports Minister Matar Ba said a young girl was among the dead.He vowed “strong measures so that such an event will never be repeated in Senegal”.Share This
