A Family Christmas Festival of Music and Carols by the Symphony Orchestra of Sri Lanka
The Concert titled “A Family Christmas Festival of Music and Carols” by the Symphony Orchestra of Sri Lanka will be held next weekend.The orchestra will be held at the Ladies College auditorium, Colombo on December 10.This year the concert features The Old Joes Choir and Contempo choirs, who together with the Symphony Orchestra of Sri Lanka will bring a special blend of Christmas favourites.The concert will feature classical “greats” like Handel’s Zadok the Priest, and Shepherd’s Farewell by Berlioz and Courtney’s Musicological Journey through the twelve days of Christmas.YES FM, Legends FM and TV 1 are the media sponsors for the concert.Share This
