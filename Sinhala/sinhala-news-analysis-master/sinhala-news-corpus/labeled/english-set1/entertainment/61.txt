Natasha Rathnayake’s Gammadda song takes top spot on SoundGimmicks #10
The Gammadda song by Natasha Rathnayake, which was written alongside the Gammadda public service campaign, has topped the SoundGimmicks #10.The chart was complied based on votes on the website that was designed to give a platform for exploring English music in South AsiaThe melodic tune sits right on top, alongside famous artists like Stigmata. Share This
