‘Chillie’ released on bail
Singer ‘Chillie,’ who was arrested and remanded on the charge of assaulting an individual, was released on bail this morning.The Kaduwela Magistrate Dhammika Hemapala ordered that the singer be released on a cash bail of Rs.10 000 , and a surety of Rs.500, 000.The case was postponed until January 24, 2017.Share This
