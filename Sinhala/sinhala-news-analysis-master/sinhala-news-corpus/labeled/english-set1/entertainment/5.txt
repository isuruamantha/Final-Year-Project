U.S. comedian Jerry Lewis dies aged 91
Jerry Lewis, the comedian and filmmaker who was adored by many, disdained by others, but unquestionably a defining figure of American entertainment in the 20th century, died on Sunday morning at his home in Las Vegas. He was 91.A family statement said he died of natural causes at his home in Las Vegas on Sunday morning.Some of the films Lewis acted was, The Nutty Professor, The Bellboy, Rock-A-Bye Baby, At War with the Army and The Ladies Man.Lewis once became the highest paid actor in Hollywood.Share This
