SL tweeps rendezvous at #TweetUpSL
Twitter users across Sri Lanka gathered today at the Maradana Trace Expert City for Tweet Up SL. The event provides a platform for the Sri Lankan twittersphere, to get acquainted with their online colleagues, up close and personal.The ‘put a name to the face’ event saw a limited audience gather, to meet and greet their fellow ‘tweeps.’ The annual event was held for the sixth year today.
