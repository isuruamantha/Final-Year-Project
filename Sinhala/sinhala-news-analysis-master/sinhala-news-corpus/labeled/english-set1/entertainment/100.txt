Miss Intercontinental Pageant contestants arrive at Stein Studios
The contestants of the 45th Miss Intercontinental Pageant arrived at the Stein Studios in Ratmalana on Wednesday, October 12.The contestants of Miss Intercontinental who have been visiting several iconic landmarks in the the country over the past few days, arrived at the Stein Studios to commence their first rehearsal for the upcoming event.The grand finale of the iconic event, which is being hosted in Sri Lanka for the first time ever, will be held at Stein Studios Ratmalana on Sunday, October 16.The grand finale will also feature international entertainers as Argentinian singer, composer and actor Patricio Arellano and Apache Indian.MTV and MBC are the exclusive electronic media partners of the glamorous pageant.Share This
