All set for SLFP’s 66th anniversary celebrations – Over 2,000 buses to be deployed
General Secretary of the SLFP, Duminda Dissanayake, says all preparations are set to hold the 66th anniversary of the Sri Lanka Freedom Party on Sunday.He added that large crowds are expected to arrive representing electorates across the country.The General Secretary added that over 2,000 buses will be deployed to transport supporters.Duminda Dissanayake further noted that invitations have been extended to everyone, including former President Mahinda Rajapaksa, for the anniversary celebrations.Share This
