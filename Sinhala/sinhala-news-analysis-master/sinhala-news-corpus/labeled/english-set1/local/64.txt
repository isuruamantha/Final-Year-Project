Canteen Owners’ Association attributes price increase in lunch packets to “polythene”
The Canteen Owners’ Association says they plan to increase the price of a lunch packet by Rs. 10.The Convener of the Sri Lanka Canteen Association, Asela Sampath, said this move was due to the increase in the price of polythene lunch sheets.He further noted that the Association would meet tomorrow to decide on price increases on other food stuff.Share This
