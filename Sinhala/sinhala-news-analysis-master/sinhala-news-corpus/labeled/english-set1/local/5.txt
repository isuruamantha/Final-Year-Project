Over 2500 police officers to receive promotions on instructions of IGP
2,599 police officers are to receive promotions based on instructions of the Inspector General of Police.According to Police Media Spokesperson SP Ruwan Gunasekara, the promotions include 2,075 police constables being promoted to the rank of sergeant, and 189 sergeants being promoted to the rank of sub inspector.The promotions will come into effect from May 31,2016.
