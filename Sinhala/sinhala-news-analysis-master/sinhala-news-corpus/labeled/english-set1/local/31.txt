School buses and vans to get priority lane on public roads from today
The Ministry of Megapolis and Western Development states that the priority lane, which was earlier allocated for buses, can now be used for school buses and vans.The ministry informed all the drivers of school vans and buses to use the priority lane from September 06 onwards for the transportation of school children.The programme was launched in order to minimise traffic congestion in Colombo.The lane is in operation in several selected routes at present.Share This
