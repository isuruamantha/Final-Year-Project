Foreign cricket team member dies in Negombo
A member of a cricket team who arrived in Sri Lanka for a cricket tournament, died in a swimming pool at a hotel in Pamunugama, Negombo.According to police, the team arrived in the country to participate in an under 17 cricket tournament organised by the Katunayake Investment Promotion Zone.The boy drowned while he was swimming in the hotel pool with four of his teammates on Tuesday evening. .The deceased was a 12-year-old resident of Gujarat.The post mortem examination is due to be conducted at the Ragama Hospital.Share This
