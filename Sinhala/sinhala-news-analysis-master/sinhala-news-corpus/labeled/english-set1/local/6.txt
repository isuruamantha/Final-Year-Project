Seven arrests made as Wilpattu National Park allows visitors ‘at any time of day’
The Wilpattu National Park is now open to visitors at any time of day.Over the course of the last one and half months, visitors were permitted only during the morning hours due to the drought experienced across the Northern and Central areas of the country.According to the Minister of Wildlife Conservation, Gamini Jayawickrama Perera, visitors will once again be able to visit Wilpattu throughout the course of the day due to the prevailing rains.Meantime, seven suspects have been arrested for attempting to enter the Wilpattu National Park illegally.Police said the suspects had attempted to enter the park while under the influence of alcohol.The suspects, who are residents of Negombo, are due to be produced before court today.Share This
