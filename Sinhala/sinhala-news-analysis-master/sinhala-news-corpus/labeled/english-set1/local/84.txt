Centre for Indo-Lanka Initiatives at Pathfinder Foundation and National Maritime Foundation sign MoU
The Centre for Indo-Lanka Initiatives at Pathfinder Foundation and National Maritime Foundation based in New Delhi, India, signed a Memorandum of Understanding to enhance bilateral cooperation between the two think-tank organisations engaged in academic research.Admiral Dr. Jayanath Colombage (Retd.), the Director of Center for Indo-Lanka Initiatives at Pathfinder Foundation and Vice Admiral Pradeep Chauhan (Retd.), the Director of NMF signed the MoU on behalf of their respective organisations.The scope of this MoU is to embark on research activities on subject areas of maritime strategy and security studies.The Indian Ocean has become the key ocean for energy, trade and food security in the 21st Century. There is a major world economic shift taking place upon the waters of this ocean as “Asian Powers” are emerging as new economic powers.The Pathfinder and National Maritime Foundation in India have planned to conduct their first bilateral conference in February 2018, focussing on developing a platform for analytical and innovative discussion on maritime security in the Indian Ocean.Share This
