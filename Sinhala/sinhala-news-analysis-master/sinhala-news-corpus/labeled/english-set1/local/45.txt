Lashing winds disrupt life in around Polonnaruwa; cause damage to property
Around 100 houses were damaged due to strong winds that blew across the Kaduruwela area in Polonnaruwa and surrounding areas, on Monday afternoon.Assistant Director of the Polonnaruwa District Disaster Management Centre, Upul Nanayakkara, stated that a number of roads have been blocked due to fallen trees.Electricity supply to certain areas have also been disrupted.According to  the police, areas as Nelumwila, Kusumgama, Hathara Ella and Manikkapatti were affected by the winds.Share This
