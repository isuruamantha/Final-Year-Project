President declares open new building complex at Poonawa Isipathana Central College
A newly-constructed building complex at the Poonawa Isipathana Central College in Anuradhapura was declared open by President Maithripala Sirisena.The building was constructed under the “Langama Pasala Hondama Pasala” project.An amount of Rs. 170 million was allocated for the construction of the building complex.
