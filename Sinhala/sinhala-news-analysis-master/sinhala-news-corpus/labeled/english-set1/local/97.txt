Water supply to Colombo and suburbs disrupted
Water supply to Colombo and its suburbs has been disrupted owing to a breakage in the main water distribution line from Kalatuwawa.The National Water Supply and Drainage Board said the water supply to Maharagama, Boralesgamuwa, Kottawa, Pannipitiya, Rukmalgama, Mattegoda, Homagama, Meepe and Padukka will be halted until 4 a.m. tomorrow.Share This
