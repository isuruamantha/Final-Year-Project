Presidential Commission on Bond Scam: Arjun Aloysius arrives at the Presidential Commission again
Arjun Aloysius arrived at the Presidential Commission to investigate into the bond issuance, again today to provide a statement to detectives.Aloysius was body searched before being entering the premises.Aloysius’s Counsel was instructed to inform Arjun Aloysius that he must prepare an affidavit and appear before the Commission on Monday, September 11.Arjun Aloysius first appeared before the Commission on Thursday and provided a statement for more than eight hours.The Presidential Commission was informed today that President’s Counsel Gamini Marapana will appear for Arjun Aloysius with effect from today.Gamini Marapana is the brother of Minister of Foreign Affairs, Tilak Marapana.Share This
